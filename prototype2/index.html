About Us

Der Deutsche Verein (German Club) is a fun and inclusive club for anyone interested in learning more about the culture, language and society in German-speaking countries. No German knowledge is required.
We participate in a variety of German cultural events as well as just have leisurely get togethers.
Wir freuen uns, wenn Sie dabei sein könnten!
