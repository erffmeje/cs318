We'd love to hear from you!!!
If you ever have an event suggestion, a question, or comment please let us know!!!

The UWEC German Club hosts a variety of events each semester. Events range from bowling to grill outs to hosting a room in a university/ community event.
We do not charge fees to attend any of the events. However, if an event does require a payment, the participant is responsible for covering the cost.
